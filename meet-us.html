About Us

Eggspression is a brand of easter eggs with expressions. Every single one of our eggs has its own personality, making the egg hunting experience a lot better and funnier. Let's get started!

MR. EGGDWARDS

Mr. Eggdwards is the oldest and the most wise egg in our community. He's often referred to as a gramps and everyone loves him and respects him.

EGGY

Eggy likes to chill and play, the best of him is that he's always got a smile on his face, and is ready to play whenever you want. He rocks!

LENNY

Lenny is a good egg, he's just a little bit of a drama king. He's just missing someone who wants to play with him. Why don't you give him a try? We're sure you'll love him as much as we do.

LOVSIE

She's the most adorable and lovable lady egg in the world. Your heart's going to melt with her. Add her to your cart and let her love you!

TEAR TOM

Tom is not sad, he's just emotional, and crying is the way he shows his feelings, if you buy him he'll cry a river of happiness. So what are you waiting for? Add him to your cart!

EGGSMILER

He's the happiest egg in the whole world, with a smile as big as nobody else's. You'll have a blast with him hunting eggs this easter and always.
